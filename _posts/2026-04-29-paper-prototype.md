Hi everyone, here's my paper prototype for my Schnapsen Cheater Pumpkin Eater. My plan is to have the LEDs, light sensor, and arduino exposed, but the buzzer and button hidden inside the pumpkin. The light senosr will be connected to pins 4 and 5 and the LEDs both to pin 6. They'll then share a ground trace. The Buzzer will be connected to pin 10 and a ground trace, and the button will be connected to pin 2 and a positive trace.
