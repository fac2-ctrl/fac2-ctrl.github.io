Hi everyone! Here's my arduino, which is flashing (despite what the image might lead you to believe). The light turns on for 1/2 a second every tenth time through the 1/4 second loop, alternating through three red, three green, and three blue. Advice I'd give for this project is to just get into the lab and put code on the arduino instead of trying to check my code by running through the loop in my notes.
