Hi everyone! Heres my homemade switches assignment. It's circuit with a coin battery and one led plus 2 homemade switches. The first is just two patches of conductive thread that can be pushed together. The second is a paperclip that slides into a flap of conductive fabric. A tip I'd give is to put the thread patches at a reasonable distance.
