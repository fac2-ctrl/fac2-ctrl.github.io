Hi everyone! Here's my random colors project! This code turns the on board RGBLED a random color when the button* is held. A tip I'd give is to think about how to have the button stay on and the same color when held.

*I also decided to use the light detector as a button so I wouldn't have to worry about aligator clips, and it works just fine.
