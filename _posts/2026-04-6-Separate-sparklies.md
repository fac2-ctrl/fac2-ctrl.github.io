Hi everyone! Here's my arduino for the separate sparklies project! The point of this project is to learn how to apply a state based aproach by simulating the behavior of fireflies in the Great Smoky Mountains. A tip I'd give my past self is to make sure you're consistent with capitalization in variable names! I spent longer than I should have double checking my code.
