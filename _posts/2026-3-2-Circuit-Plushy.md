Hi! Here's My circuit plushy. Just a note, my circuit is flipped from my prototype because of how I traced my diagram, but it still functions the same.

The goal of this project was to build a plushy from felt that contains a circuit with 3 LEDs, a button, a switch, and a battery. A tip that I'd give is to not underestimate how tempermental the conductive thread can be. It can be hard to secure you're ends especially since you have to cut them quite short to avoid internal connections.
